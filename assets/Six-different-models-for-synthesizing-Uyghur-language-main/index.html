Zero-shot Speech Synthesis for Uyghur Language among Six Different Models

This webpage provides a detailed comparison of six speech synthesis models. The models are evaluated based on audio samples produced by each model using the same input text. Users can listen to the samples and assess the differences in speech quality across different models. The models included are FastSpeech2, Grad-TTS, vits1, vits2, RAW, and XLMR, representing various approaches in the field of speech synthesis. Below you can find audio samples of each model, showcasing their unique characteristics.
