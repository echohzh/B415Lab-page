Zero-shot Speech Synthesis for Uyghur Language among Three Different Models

This demo mainly includes zero-shot speech synthesis for the Uyghur language under three different models, including Fastspeech2, YourTTS, and MDDM, and conducted experiments among Chinese, English, and Uyghur languages. This paper presents the synthesis effects of different models on multiple speech samples and conducts a detailed comparative analysis.
